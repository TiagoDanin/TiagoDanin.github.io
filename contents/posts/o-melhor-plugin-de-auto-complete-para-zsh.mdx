One of the greatest wonders of using ZSH is its customization flexibility, with a large number of plugins developed by users.

About a year ago (in 2017) I found the best plugin — zsh-autosuggestions. Its main feature is the display of previously typed commands in the form of a "muted gray" color.

Just like code editors such as Atom and Sublime, using the "tab" key you can complete the sequence of characters already present in the history file, in our case that would be "zhistory". The productivity boost is noticeable in the first few days.
